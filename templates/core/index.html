About - Gamer Stats

What is Gamer Stats?

Gamer Stats is a free to use online database built for gamers by gamers. The primary focus of this website is to track live esports games (coming soon!), competitive matches, professional players, streamers news and rumours.

Gamer Stats also covers the PC Specs, equipment, and streaming setup of CS:GO, VALORANT, DOTA 2, and LoL's esports players as well as popular Twitch streamer and content creators from around the globe.

When Gamer Stats was started?

Gamer Stats was started in 2019, somewhere around the month of August and September. I (admin) am a full stack developer, I created this for posting my own gaming stories but later I turned that Gamer Stats into this, which you're seeing right now with the help of my cousin.

Who is working behind the website right now?

My brother left after 3 months (approx.) in the end of 2019 as he moved to another city for a job and didn't had much time to put into this. But he did helped me to put a lot of things for the website.

Right now, me and my neighbor is working on this. Recently, we created this new version of the website for better usability and accessibility.

How do you collect PC Specs of players and are they correct?

We generally track them from their live streamers and follow them on all social media. We try to update them as soon as possible whenever someone change their specs or gaming equipements but if you notice any error then you can help us and submit the updated information using the form on our contact page.

What if I need help or want contact you?

If you have any problem (anything related to the website or content) or want to send us your feedback. Then you can use the form on our contact page to submit your query.
